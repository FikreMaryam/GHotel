About Us

Welcome to GHotel, a place where history meets modern elegance. Established in the early 1900s, GHotel has been a symbol of timeless charm and warm hospitality.

From our grand lobby with vintage architecture to our thoughtfully designed guest rooms, every corner of GHotel reflects our dedication to comfort and excellence. Whether you are here for business, a family vacation, or a romantic getaway, we promise an unforgettable stay.

Our Mission

At GHotel, our mission is simple: to create memorable experiences for every guest by blending the elegance of tradition with the comfort of modern living.
